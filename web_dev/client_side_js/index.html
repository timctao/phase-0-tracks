Welcome to my childhood obsession: Pokémon!

Why do I like it?

With Pokémon Go recently taking over the world by storm, I wanted to let everyone know why I personally enjoy Pokémon and, after so many years, keep coming back again and again to continue to enjoy it.

  • It provides an escape from reality.

    • Often times I find myself needing a break from whatever life throws at me, and Pokémon always gave me some sort of relief from those responsibiltiies.

  • It appeals to the collector in me.

    • I've always enjoyed collecting cards or figurines and the way Pokémon was designed was to appeal to people like me. The hunt never ends.

  • Nostalgia.

    • Growing up I thought one day I would grow out of these things I deemed "childish," but it is the very fact that Pokémon was so much apart of that childhood that it makes me want to keep coming back to see old Pokémon and discover new ones.

  • Some are pretty darn cute.

    • Need I say any more?
